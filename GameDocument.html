Game summary

Our game ACE: skies unknown is 3D video action network-based game. It is an android based game project. In this project, there are four main components to be implemented, • Networking • Graphics • Game Code. Fighter pilots operate in environments where an erroneous decision may have fatal consequences, Analyzing the situations and making correct decisions in fraction of seconds is the toughest part. This project is about air combat survival, After playing the game, the player gets a lot of knowledge about Fighting Jets How they are used in air wars, their major features. It increases the player decisional powers.
